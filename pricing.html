FAQ

Pricing Questions

Can I try Pro features before buying?

Yes! We offer a free trial of Pro features so you can experience unlimited scanning and all six scan modes before committing. No credit card required to start.

What happens when my free tier limits are reached?

When you reach the 1,000 file or 10 GB data limit, you can still view your scan history and previously flagged files. To run new scans, you'll need to upgrade to Pro.

Is the license tied to one machine?

Yes, each Pro license is bound to one machine via a hardware fingerprint. Enterprise plans support multiple seats. If you need to transfer your license to a new machine, contact support.

Do you offer refunds?

No, we don't offer refunds.
